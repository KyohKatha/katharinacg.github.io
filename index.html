I'm a 3D Character Animator and Lead Animator at ICON Creative Studios, based in the beautiful West Kelowna, BC, Canada.

I've had the amazing opportunity to breathe life into characters on shows like Super Monsters, The Rocketeer, Action Pack, Alice's Wonderland Bakery, and Ariel. Each project has been a playground for storytelling through movement!

3D Character Animation

Performance, body mechanics, acting, and polish — making characters feel alive and believable.

Love Challenging Shots

Complexity, problem-solving, and iteration — the tougher the shot, the more fun it is!

Detail-Oriented

Meticulous with notes, consistency, and multi-tasking — nothing slips through the cracks.
